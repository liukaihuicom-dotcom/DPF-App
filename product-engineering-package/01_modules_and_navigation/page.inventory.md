# Page Inventory

| Page ID | Platform | Primary Role | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| `funding_home` | App/H5 | Trader | Show funding actions, balances, risk status, and recent transactions. |
| `deposit_form` | App/H5 | Trader | Submit IDR deposit to a selected USD trading account. |
| `deposit_detail` | App/H5 | Trader | Show deposit payment instructions, status, FX, fee, and ledger outcome. |
| `withdrawal_form` | App/H5 | Trader | Submit IDR withdrawal from a selected USD trading account. |
| `withdrawal_detail` | App/H5 | Trader | Show withdrawal review, payout, fee, FX, and final result. |
| `transfer_form` | App/H5 | Trader | Transfer USD between same-owner active trading accounts. |
| `transfer_detail` | App/H5 | Trader | Show transfer status, source/target, audit, and ledger outcome. |
| `funding_transaction_list` | App/H5 | Trader | List deposits, withdrawals, and transfers. |
| `funding_transaction_detail` | App/H5 | Trader | Unified transaction detail for support and audit. |
| `admin_funding_review_list` | Admin | Funding Reviewer | Review queue with filters, risk flags, and SLA. |
| `admin_funding_review_detail` | Admin | Funding Reviewer / Risk Compliance | Approve or reject manual review items. |
| `admin_funding_channel_config` | Admin | Internal Operator | Configure channel availability and maintenance state. |
| `admin_funding_limit_fee_config` | Admin | Risk Compliance / Internal Operator | Configure min/max/daily/monthly/fee/fx policy. |
| `admin_funding_exception_reconciliation` | Admin | Risk Compliance | Investigate provider, ledger, FX, and amount mismatches. |
