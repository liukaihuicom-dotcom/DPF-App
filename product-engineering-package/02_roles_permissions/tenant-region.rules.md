# Tenant And Region Rules

- Region scope is Indonesia (`ID`) for V1.
- Customer-facing payment and payout currency is IDR.
- Trading account ledger currency is USD.
- Region-specific provider names, bank lists, e-wallet brands, cut-off times, and local regulatory text are not hardcoded in this package.
- Any expansion beyond Indonesia requires new region rules, payment method availability, copy review, risk review, and test mapping.
