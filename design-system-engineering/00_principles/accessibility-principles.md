# Accessibility Principles

Source of truth: `design-system/07-quality/accessibility-checklist.md`.

## Baseline

- Interactive controls must expose role, label, disabled, loading, and selected state where applicable.
- Touch targets must satisfy the platform minimum defined in runtime tokens.
- Error, empty, loading, success, and restricted states must remain perceivable without relying on color alone.
- Financial risk and compliance information must not be hidden for visual simplicity.
